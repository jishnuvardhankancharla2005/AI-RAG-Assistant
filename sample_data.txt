# CyberCorp Inc. - Employee Handbook 2026

## 1. Remote Work Policy
Employees at CyberCorp Inc. are allowed to work remotely up to 3 days a week. The remaining 2 days must be spent in the office. 
Core working hours are between 10:00 AM and 3:00 PM (EST), during which all employees must be online and available for team meetings.
Equipment stipends of $500 per year are provided for home office setups.

## 2. Leave Policy
All full-time employees are entitled to 20 days of paid time off (PTO) per year. In addition, there are 10 paid public holidays. 
Sick leave is unlimited, but any sickness lasting more than 3 consecutive days requires a doctor's note.
Parental leave allows up to 16 weeks of fully paid time off for both primary and secondary caregivers.

## 3. Security Guidelines
All passwords must be at least 16 characters long and include a mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters. 
Passwords must be changed every 90 days.
Access to the production database requires Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) and is strictly limited to the DevOps and Senior Engineering teams.
In case of a suspected data breach, employees must immediately contact the Security Operations Center (SOC) at soc@cybercorp.example.com.

## 4. Project "Project Titan"
Project Titan is an upcoming enterprise cybersecurity suite designed to integrate AI-driven threat detection. 
The expected launch date for the beta version is Q3 2026. 
The lead engineer for Project Titan is Sarah Connor, and the product manager is John Smith. 
Key features include:
- Real-time Phishing analysis
- Automated Malware isolation
- AI-based user behavior anomaly detection
