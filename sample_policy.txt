ACME Corp Remote Work & Cybersecurity Policy
Effective Date: January 1, 2026

1. Remote Work Guidelines:
All employees are eligible for up to 3 days of remote work per week. Remote work days must be approved by the direct manager at least one week in advance. Employees must ensure they have a stable internet connection and a quiet environment for meetings.

2. Cybersecurity Protocols:
- VPN Requirement: When working remotely, all employees MUST connect to the ACME Corp internal network using the standard company VPN (Cisco AnyConnect).
- Device Usage: Only company-issued laptops may be used to access internal databases and source code. Personal devices are strictly prohibited.
- Phishing Awareness: Employees must complete the mandatory anti-phishing training course by Q2 of each year. Suspicious emails should be reported immediately to the IT Security desk at security@acmecorp.com.

3. Data Classification:
- Public Data: Marketing materials and press releases. Can be shared freely.
- Internal Data: Employee directories, company newsletters. Do not share outside the company.
- Confidential Data: Source code, financial reports, and customer PII (Personally Identifiable Information). Requires encrypted transfer and strict access controls.
